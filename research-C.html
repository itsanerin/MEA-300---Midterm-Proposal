Research Site C

Since J. Black identifies as an Indie Blues musician, my third research site is Making a Scene, the official website of the independent music magazine of the same name. It calls itself "the #1 resource for the independent artist and the fans that love them." It lives up to this by featuring news, podcasts, interviews, information, and advertising for all things in the independent music scene, and even has its own section for the Indie Blues genre.
