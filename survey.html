Survey the Field

The target audiences of this website will be fans, potential fans, and venues/companies/individuals who are interested in hiring a musician. Fans and potential fans will be able to access all of J. Black's social media links, listen to his Soundcloud tracks, and find out when and where they can see his upcoming performances. Potential clients can listen to various samples of his original work, get a glimpse of his stage presence from the videos in the Gallery, and contact him directly.
My Research Site A covers the aspects of a musician's website that would best service these audiences.

Another site (HubSpot Marketing's “45 Visual Content Marketing Statistics You Should Know in 2018”), however, was able to provide some useful information as to what would draw users to my site.

"- 32% of marketers say visual images are the most important form of content for their business, with blogging in second (27%).

- When people hear information, they're likely to remember only 10% of that information three days later. However, if a relevant image is paired with that same information, people retained 65% of the information three days later.

- 11% more B2C marketers than B2B marketers say visual content is the most important type of content today.

- Cisco projects that global internet traffic from videos will make up 82% of all consumer internet traffic by 2021."

This information encourages me to rely heavily on visual elements and images for my site, rather than walls of text. I will keep this in mind in deciding how to rework the About/Bio page.

Upon conducting research that delved deeper into my target audience and building websites in general, I realized I should have optimized my site to appear to target users on online search engines.

Out of the possible search terms most relevant to my site, "live music near me" and "blues music" were the most commonly searched in New York City, where J. Black is based. As I continue to develop this site in the future, the importance of search terms is something I'd like to better integrate. Google trends is a tool I'd like to carry forward, and I will definitely keep it is mind the next time I attempt to reach out to an audience on a digital platform.
