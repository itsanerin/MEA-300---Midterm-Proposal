Survey the Field

The target audiences of this website will be fans, potential fans, and venues/companies/individuals who are interested in hiring a musician. Fans and potential fans will be able to access all of J. Blak's social media links, listen to his Soundcloud tracks, find out when and where they can see his next performances, subscribe to his email list. Potential clients can listen to various samples of his original work, see his availability, and contact him directly.
