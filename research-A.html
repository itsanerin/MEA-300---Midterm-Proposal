Research Site A

The first website I found to be useful is an article on the site, DIY Musician, titled 10 things every musician website must have." It is incredibly helpful to me in gathering the content mapping out the pages I'll need to make an effective muscian's website.

The 10 components are:

  1. An updated About page with multiple versions of your bio
  2. A press page with hi-res photos, press quotes, and your latest press release
  3. A music player
  4. Videos
  5. Social Media
  6. Contact Info
  7. A concert calendar
  8. A news section or blog
  9. A music and/or merch store
  10. Email capture tool
